## üìê DESIGN GUIDELINES SUMMARY

### Logo Usage
- Maintain clear space around logo
- Use primary logo for vertical spaces
- Use secondary logo for horizontal spaces
- Do not distort or recolor outside brand palette

### Color Application
- Purple as dominant color for authority
- Pink for warmth and approachability
- White for cleanliness and space
- Secondary colors for variety and softness

### Typography Hierarchy
- Headlines: Bold, large, attention-grabbing
- Subheads: Medium weight, clear location/service info
- Body: Regular weight, readable, informative

### Photography Style
- Professional healthcare imagery
- Friendly, smiling staff
- Clean, modern clinic environment
- Family-oriented scenarios
- Soft lighting, warm tones
